Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our URL shortening service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

  • The short URL: The shortened URL created by you.
  • The Long URL: The original URL that you provide for shortening.
  • Create time: The date and time when the URL was created.
  • User Information: If you are logged in, we store your username. If you are not logged in, we store "Guest".
  • Registration and Session Data: We store data related to your registration and sessions.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used for the purpose of providing and improving our URL shortening service, and for managing user registrations and sessions.

3. Sharing Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to comply with the law or respond to legal requests.

4. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect the information stored in our database.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to request deletion of shortened URLs that violate our site's policies or if the destination page causes harm. Users must provide proof that the URL points to them personally to request deletion under these conditions. To exercise these rights, please contact us through the details provided on our contact page.

6. Administrative Control

Website administrators have full control to delete links created by any user to maintain the integrity and security of the service.
