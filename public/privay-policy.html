Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our URL shortening service.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the following information when you use our service:

  • The short URL: The shortened URL created by you.
  • The Long URL: The original URL that you provide for shortening.
  • Create time: The date and time when the URL was created.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving our URL shortening service.

3. Sharing Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to comply with the law or respond to legal requests.

4. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect the information stored in our database.

5. User Rights

Since no URL shortening is directly linked to any individual, deletions of shortened URLs can only be requested if they violate our site's policies or if the destination page causes harm. Users must provide proof that the URL points to them personally to request deletion under these conditions. To exercise these rights, please contact us through the details provided on our contact page.

6. Administrative Control

Website administrators have full control to delete links created by any user to maintain the integrity and security of the service.
